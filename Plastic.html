Reduce Plastic Usage

Take action and make a difference by minimizing your plastic usage with these tips:

1. Use Reusable Bags

Bring your reusable shopping bags to reduce plastic bag waste.

2. Say No to Single-Use Plastic

Avoid single-use plastics like straws, utensils, and water bottles.

3. Choose Sustainable Packaging

Opt for products with eco-friendly packaging and materials.

4. Participate in Cleanups

Join beach cleanup events and help remove plastic waste from our shores.
